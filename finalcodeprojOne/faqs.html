Got a Question? Check here!
What if my mobility is limited?
Patina caters to all forms of mobility issues through our personalized activities. If mobility is an issue in your day to day life, Patina will only relay wheelchair friendly activities which will be gauged by your survey answers, furthermore step by step GPS navigation is also offered through our website.
Does it cost anything to become a member?
No. Patina is a non profit organization that acts as a third party platform which puts users in touch with local and engaging activities. Activities offered through our website range from free or may but subject to a cost. The activities which appear in your personalized activity calendar are based on your answers provided in the initial survey.
What if I have nobody to go to an activity with?
Patina is a service which aims to empower the elderly and connect them with other users with similar interests. Members are encouraged to discover new activities on their own or with friends. Whether you're alone or in a group, Patina is an environment where new friendships can be made while leading an active lifestyle.
What's the age limit?
There is no age limit but generally Patina caters to golden agers between the age of 55 and 80.
How do I get started?
Becoming a member is as simple as answering a series of questions in order to gauge individual interests, mobility and location. Upon completion, you are then able to browse local activities and start your Patina experience!
