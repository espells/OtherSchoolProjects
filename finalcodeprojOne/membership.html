Becoming a Member at Patina
STEP 1:
Upon signing up with Patina, users undergo a series of questionnaires with the goal of creating a personalized profile. Once the membership is activated, participants will be able to find nearby activities based on their interests and location through our easy to use website.
STEP 2:
Becoming a member means having premium access to hundreds of activities in the greater Montreal area which are tailored to individual needs and interests. Members are offered the opportunity to learn new skills or rediscover a hobby all while connecting with other senior citizens with similar interests.
STEP 3:
Start using Patina to find events that fit your needs and interests!
